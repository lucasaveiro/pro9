Project Example: The Custom Fabrication Shop

The Problem

Jobs were written on paper "travelers" that got oily and lost. The owner had to walk the floor to find job statuses. Invoices went out weeks late.

The Solution

We built a "Job Tracker" Web App. Tablets on the floor let welders tap "Start" and "Finish".

The Results

  • Visibility: Owner sees status of every job from his phone.
  • Cash Flow: Invoices generate automatically upon shipping.
  • Sanity: No more shouting across the shop floor.
